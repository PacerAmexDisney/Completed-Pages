Learn from Experts: A guide to partnering with your child’s physician for better results

Going Beyond Medicine: How your child’s physician can support wellness and recovery for your child with mental health, emotional, or behavioral needs

Tuesday, April 18, 2017 from 6:30 to 8:30 p.m.

Location: PACER Center

Presented by Dr. Barry Garfinkel and Dr. Richard Peterson. Barry Garfinkel, M.D. is president and founder of the Center for Developmental Psychopharmacology and a noted child and adolescent psychiatrist. Richard Peterson, M.D. is a child and adolescent psychiatrist with Fairview Health Services, and an assistant professor at the University of Minnesota Department of Psychiatry.

Parents of children or youth with mental health, emotional, or behavioral needs frequently go to their child's doctor for help. This workshop will inform parents how to partner with their doctor by better preparing for the visit. They will learn what role medications may play in treatment and how to apply that information for positive outcomes in their home, at school, and in their community.
