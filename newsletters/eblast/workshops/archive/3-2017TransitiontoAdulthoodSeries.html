Preparing for Transition to Adulthood Workshop Series

Transition: Pathways to the Future - Using the IEP to Develop Transition Services

Thursday, February 16, 2017 from 6:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m.

This workshop will help parents of youth with disabilities understand how to use the Individualized Education Program (IEP) to develop transition services that support their youth in planning for life after high school. Parents of students in the eighth grade and beyond are encouraged to attend.

This workshop is funded in part by a grant from the Minnesota Department of Education.

Register online

Location: Rosemount

ABCs of the IEP for Transition-age Students and Their Parents

Tuesday, March 7, 2017 from 6:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m.

The Individuals with Disabilities Education Act (IDEA) requires schools to invite youth with disabilities to attend the IEP meeting for transition planning. This workshop will help students with IEPs in eighth grade and higher and their parents understand how to use the IEP to assist with the transition to adulthood.

Register online

Location: Inver Grove Heights
