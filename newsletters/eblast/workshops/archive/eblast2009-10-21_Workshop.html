Simon Technology Center 2009 Open House

Come See What’s Cool! This 22nd annual event highlights cutting-edge assistive technologies, including options for improving communication, computer accessibility, education, literacy, recreation, vision, and more.

  • Come talk with vendors.
  • Sample assistive technology.
  • Attend hands-on computer sessions on several topics, including social networking and Internet safety for kids.
  • Register for prizes.
  • Have fun with accessible art projects, a magic show, adapted bikes, and other great activities.

Haven’t visited the Simon Technology Center before?

Join us to:
  • meet technology center staff
  • take a tour of the technology library
  • learn how to borrow materials to take home
  • find out more about PACER Center and program offerings

Directions to PACER Center:
From I-494 take the Normandale Boulevard exit south to 84th Street; turn left.
Take the next left onto the frontage road.
PACER Center is a one-story brick building immediately north of the bridge.
Turn right into the parking lot. Enter on the north side of the building.
