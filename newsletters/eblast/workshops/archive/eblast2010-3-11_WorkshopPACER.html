IDEA: Understanding the Special Education Process

Thursday, March 11, 2010 from 6:30pm - 9:30pm - see the flyer

The Individuals with Disabilities Education Act (IDEA) and its regulations affect how children in special education are served. This workshop will cover topics including: understanding special education, Individualized Education Programs (IEPs), keeping special education records, and how to become an effective advocate for your child.
