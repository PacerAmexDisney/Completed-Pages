Shakopee Public Schools Second Annual Transition Expo

For families of students ages 12-21
who are on an Individualized Education Program (IEP)
and planning for their future after high school

Learn from local experts about the supports, services, and programs that may be available for young adults in the areas of employment, transportation, college supports, social security, financial and legal planning, recreation, health care, guardianship, and education. Attend information sessions, visit with agency representatives and service providers, and receive helpful publications and resources.

Breakout information sessions − 6 p.m.

  • Kathie Erickson, Work Experience Coordinator − Student Panel on Developing Employment Skills
  • PACER Center Staff - Exploring Transition Options: How to find the services and supports needed after high school

Breakout information sessions − 7 p.m.

  • Carver-Scott Educational Cooperative − Transition Program Options
  • Anne Swanberg, Dakota County Technical College − Accommodations in Post-secondary Education
  • PACER Center Staff - Exploring Transition Options (repeat)


This event is sponsored by the Shakopee Special Services Department and SEPAC in collaboration with Scott County and PACER Center. Funding is provided through a grant from the Minnesota Department of Education.
