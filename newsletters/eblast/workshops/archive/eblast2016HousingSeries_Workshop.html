Three-Part Housing Workshop Series

Free PACER workshops for parents of teens and young adults with disabilities.

Plan to attend all three housing workshops in this series.

Part 1 – First Steps to Independent Living

Monday, Feb. 8, 2016 from 7pm - 9pm - see the flyer

This interactive workshop will focus on person-centered planning concepts and tools to help your son or daughter develop their vision for independent living, housing, and services. One-page profiles will be shared and how they were helpful in starting to explore housing and services. An overview of the housing and services options will also be provided.  Register online

Location: PACER Center

Part 2 – Housing Eligibility and Funding

Monday, Feb. 22, 2016 from 7pm - 9pm - see the flyer

Parents will gain a big picture view of how housing is funded through federal, state, and county resources, and learn how their son or daughter can become eligible for and access funding. Topics include income supplements and budgets. Register online

Location: PACER Center

Part 3 – Individualized Housing & Services Options

Monday, March 7, 2016 from 7pm - 9pm - see the flyer

This interactive workshop will provide a more detailed explanation of individualized housing and services options so that parents and young adults can consider what might be a good fit for them. A panel of parents, young adults, and housing providers will share their journey through the housing process. Register online

Location: PACER

 

To find out more about the Housing project at PACER, please visit PACER.org/housing
