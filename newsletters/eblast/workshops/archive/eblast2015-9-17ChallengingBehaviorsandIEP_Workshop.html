Challenging Behaviors and the Individualized Education Program (IEP): Making a plan that works for children and youth

Thursday, Sept. 17, from 6:30 to 8:30 p.m.

Children and youth with challenging behaviors often struggle to be successful in school. This workshop will discuss what challenging behavior in school may look like, why those behaviors are so hard to change, and offer suggestions on how to use the Individualized Education Program (IEP) to make a plan that works to support school success.

Topics include:

  • Understanding how challenging behaviors link to supports and services in the IEP
  • Identifying effective strategies for collaborating with the IEP team
  • Learning how to use evaluation and assessment data for better student outcomes
  • Tips for increasing youth driven IEP’s
