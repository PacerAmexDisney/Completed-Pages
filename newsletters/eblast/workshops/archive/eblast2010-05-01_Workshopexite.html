Apply now for EX.I.T.E. Camp 2010 - Deadline is May 1, 2010

EXploring Interests in Technology and Engineering

Attention middle-school girls with disabilities! Roll up your sleeves and prepare to get messy with science. EX.I.T.E. camp will have you exploring, discovering, creating, and challenging yourself to investigate the world around you through technology.

  • Experience the excitement surrounding science, technology, engineering, and math.
  • Hear professionals with disabilities talk about their success in technology fields.
  • Learn through hands-on experiments, such as: making music videos, programming robots to dance, making ice cream, and more.
  • Meet new people and have fun.

Is EX.I.T.E for you?

EX.I.T.E Camp is for girls with disabilities who:

  • will enter grades 6-9 during the 2010-2011 school year
  • like science
  • work well in a hands-on environment

When is camp?

Camp is Aug. 9, 11, 13, 17, and 19. See PACER.org/stc/exite/camp.asp for camp schedule.

  • Opening and closing ceremonies are Aug. 5 and Aug. 19.

What does it cost?

  • Camp is free!
  • Girls need to bring their own bag lunch each day and provide their own transportation to and from camp.

How do I sign up?

For more information & to register Click Here

The deadline is May 1, 2010. Applications are accepted on a first come, first served basis. If space remains, returning campers will be entered into a drawing. Letters notifying applicants of their status will be mailed in early June.
