EX.I.T.E. Camp

(EXploring Interests in Technology and Engineering)

Middle school girls with disabilities, roll up your sleeves and prepare to get messy with science, meet new friends, and have an amazing experience during a free, 5-day summer camp. EX.I.T.E. camp will teach you how to explore, discover, create, and challenge yourself to investigate the world around you.

  • A 5-Day Camp- Aug. 4, 6, 8, 12, 14. Opening and closing ceremonies are July 31 and Aug. 14 respectively.
  • Camp runs each day from 9 a.m. to 4 p.m. at PACER Center in Bloomington.
  • It's FREE, you just need transportation and a bag lunch each day.

EX.I.T.E Camp is for girls with disabilities entering grades 6-9 during the 2013-14 school year.
