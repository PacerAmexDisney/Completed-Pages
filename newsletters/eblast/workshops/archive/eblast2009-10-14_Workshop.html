IDEA: Understanding the Special Education Process

The Individuals with Disabilities Education Act (IDEA) and its regulations affect how children in special education are to be served.

This workshop is for parents of children with disabilities and for professionals.

It will cover basic information on such topics as:

  • Understanding special education
  • Understanding Individualized Education Programs (IEPs)
  • Evaluating and re-evaluating students
  • Keeping special education records
  • Becoming an effective advocate for your child and effective IEP team member
  • Resolving differences

Each participant will receive a special education record-keeping folder.
