Upcoming PACER Workshops

Free PACER workshops for parents of children with disabilities.

Social Security for Transition-Age Youth

Wednesday, Sept. 21, 2011 from 1pm-3pm - see the flyer

Join PACER Center for this free workshop for parents of children w/disabilities ages 14 and older. You will learn about Social Security disability programs, Supplemental Security Income, Work incentives, the Plan to Achieve Self-Support program (PASS), and Ticket to Work program. Co-sponsored by PACER Center and the Social Security Administration. Register online

Location: Rochester

Transition: Pathway to the Future

Tuesday, Sept. 27, 2011 from 6:30pm - 8:30pm - see the flyer

This workshop will help parents of children with disabilities understand how to use the special education transition process to help plan for life after high school. Parents of students in the ninth grade and beyond are encouraged to attend. Register online

Location: Red Wing
