Upcoming PACER Workshops

Free PACER workshops for parents of young children with disabilities.

Talk the Talk

 Thursday, September 16, 2010 from 6:30pm to 8:30pm - see the flyer

 Parents need to have an active role in early intervention and early childhood special education services for their children. In this workshop, you will learn: the importance of your role when working with early childhood professionals, how to prepare for an IFSP/IEP meeting, how to identify your child's strengths and needs, and the skills and strategies to use to more effectively communicate with them Register online

Location: PACER Center

Engaged Parents Equal Successful Children

Thursday, September 23, 2010 from 6:30pm to 8:30pm  - see the flyer

 Parents are their child's first teacher. This workshop will provide parents of children with developmental delays or disabilities from birth to age 7, with ideas on how to be active participants in their child's early education and set the foundation for future learning.Register online

Location: PACER Center
