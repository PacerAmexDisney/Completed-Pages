Free PACER workshops for parents of children with disabilities.

Special Education: What Do I Need to Know?

Hmong, Spanish, and Somali versions of this workshop will be presented concurrently. 

Thursday, October 07, 2010 from 5:30pm to 8:30pm

Special Education is instruction designed specifically to meet the requirements of children with special needs. It is provided in the most appropriate setting for the children and at no cost to parents or families. This workshop will cover topics such as: Why is it important for parents to be involved? How can I work effectively with the school? What do I need to know about the evaluation process? How do we make the Individualized Education Program (IEP) fit the child? How do special education and regular education work together?

There will NOT be an English version of this workshop presented on this evening, but please join us for IDEA: Blueprint for Special Education.

IDEA: Blueprint for Special Education is being offered in English on October 7, 2010, from 5:30-8:30pm, at the Anoka-Hennepin Staff Development Center, 2727 N. Ferry Street, Anoka, MN 55303.
