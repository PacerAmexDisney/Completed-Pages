Open House: Creating Community Connections for Children and Youth with Mental Health and Behavioral Challenges

Monday, Nov. 2, 2015 from 5 to 7 p.m.

PACER Center’s Children’s Mental Health and Emotional or Behavioral Disorders Project is hosting an open house event for families..

Providers of community-based resources, services, and supports will be on hand to offer information about the services they provide to children or youth with mental health, emotional, or behavioral challenges. Participants will have an opportunity to network with other parents and professionals. Participants will also hear the PACER Youth Advisory Board talk about their experiences as teens with mental health challenges.

Pizza will be served.
