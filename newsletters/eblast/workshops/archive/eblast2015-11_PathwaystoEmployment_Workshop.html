PACER Center workshops

Pathways to Employment

Monday, November 16, 2015 from 7:00 p.m. to 8:30 p.m.

What comes next after high school? This workshop is designed to help parents work with their son or daughter to map out their own, individual, career pathway. Parents will gain knowledge about how to help prepare their youth for employment, and receive Vocational Rehabilitation Services and Social Security Income (SSI) as appropriate.

Parents of students in the 9th grade and beyond are encouraged to attend. Register online

Location: Moorhead
