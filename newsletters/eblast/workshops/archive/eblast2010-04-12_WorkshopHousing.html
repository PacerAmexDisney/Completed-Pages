Housing and Services: Putting the Pieces Together

Monday, April 12, 2010 from 7:00pm to 9:30pm

Learn how to creatively combine housing options and supports that will help your young adult with a disability live as independently as possible. Parents will learn how a variety of housing models, services, and supports can be tailored to the their youth with a disability. By reviewing three real-life stories illustrating decisions made by people with developmental and mental health disabilities, participants will gain a clear picture of how housing options can work.

Housing professionals, young adults, and their families will share stories about:

  • how person-centered thinking helped define their independent living decisions
  • which financial resources addressed their support needs and chosen housing option
  • how they creatively combined services and supports in licensed and unlicensed settings

Speakers include:

  • Young adult and parent panel
  • Catherine DeVaan, principal planning analyst, Hennepin Housing and Homeless Initiatives
  • Jerry Mellum, senior planning analyst, Hennepin County Aging and Disability Services
  • Sara Yee and Trevor Motley, program directors, Pinnacle Services, Inc.
