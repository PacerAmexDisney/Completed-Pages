Upcoming PACER Workshops

Free PACER workshops for parents of children with disabilities.

Special Time with an Advocate

 Thursday, Sept. 16, 2010 and Thursday, Oct. 28, 2010
3:15 to 8 p.m.- see the flyer

 Parents can take advantage of this opportunity to meet face to face with a PACER Center advocate. An English and Spanish-speaking advocate will be available during parent/teacher conferences. Parents can use this time to ask the advocate any questions related to special education and discuss their child’s learning. The advocate can help parents develop a profile of their child that includes information they want school staff to know about him or her. This would be great information for parents to share at their next Individualized Education Program (IEP) meeting.

Location: (New Hope - see Map)
