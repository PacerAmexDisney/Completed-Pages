Join us for an exciting opportunity to meet and hear from Michael Yudin. He is Assistant Secretary for the Office of Special Education and Rehabilitative Services at the U.S. Department of Education.

Registration is required as space is limited. Register Online or by phone at 952-838-9000.

When: Tuesday, Aug. 20, 2013

Where: PACER Center, 8161 Normandale Blvd., Minneapolis, MN 55437

Reception 5:15 to 6:15 p.m.

You are welcome to join us at 5:15 p.m. with an informal reception. The reception is open to all.

Presentation 6:30 to 8 p.m.

The Presentation will cover information related to education of children and youth with disabilities. Michael K. Yudin is currently the Acting Assistant Secretary for the Office of Special Education and Rehabilitative Services (OSERS) at the U.S. Department of Education. He serves as principal adviser to the Secretary on matters related to the education of children and youth with disabilities, as well as employment and community living for youth and adults with disabilities. 

 
Directions to PACER Center: - (see the map)

Turn right into the parking lot. Enter on the north side of the building. Overflow parking in surrounding hotels.
