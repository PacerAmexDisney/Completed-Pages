Parent Talk: Communicating with professionals
and connecting with other families

Monday, Sept. 29, 2014 | 6:30 to 8:30 p.m.

A free PACER workshop for parents of children with developmental delays and disabilities ages birth to 5

This workshop will provide parents with tips on how to talk with early childhood professionals; participants will have the opportunity to talk and connect with other parents to share experiences and tips learned along the way.

Participants will learn:

  • The importance of a parent’s role when working with early childhood professionals
  • Skills and strategies to communicate more effectively with early childhood professionals
  • Strategies that other families have found to be helpful when communicating with professional
