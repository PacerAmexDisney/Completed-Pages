Planning Ahead for Graduation and Life

Tuesday, October 05, 2010 from 6:00pm to 8:30pm

This workshop is designed for parents of students in high school with disabilities. Topics wil include: how to create a vision for the future, what skills youth with disabilities need in order to succeed after graduation, how to use the IEP as a playbook for successful transition, and who plays a key role on the transition team.
