Upcoming PACER Workshops

Free PACER workshops for parents of children with disabilities.

IDEA: Fall Kick Off: IDEA and the IEP

 Tuesday, September 28, 2010 from 6:30pm to 9 pm - see the flyer

 This new PACER workshop is the second of a two-part series on the special education process and the IEP. The focus of this session is IDEA and the IEP. Hear an overview of the requirements of the Individualized Education Program (IEP). Visit individual stations to learn more about evaluation, progress reports, preparing for IEP meetings, understanding accommodations and modifications and more! Register online

Location: PACER Center

IDEA: Understanding the Special Education Process

Monday, October 04, 2010 from 6:30pm to 9:30pm  - see the flyer

 The Individuals with Disabilities Education Act (IDEA) and its regulations affect how children in special education are to be served. Come learn about understanding special education, understanding IEP's, evaluating students, keeping special education records, becoming an effective advocate for your child, and resolving differences.Register online

Location: St. Paul

Exploring the Invisible Disability of FASD

Tuesday, October 05, 2010 from 6:30pm to 9:00pm  - see the flyer

Join the staff from the Minnesota Organization on Fetal Alcohol Syndrome (MOFAS) as they discuss the basics of Fetal Alcohol Spectrum Disorders.Register online

Location: PACER
