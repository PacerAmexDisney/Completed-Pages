A presentation by national experts on dyslexia and Co-directors of the Yale Center for Dyslexia & Creativity

The Paradox of Dyslexia: Slow Readers and Fast Thinkers

Wed., June 7, 2017 from 7:00 to 9:00 p.m.

Location: Bloomington

The Drs. Shaywitz will discuss screening, diagnosis, evidence-based interventions, and new educational approaches. They will talk about the importance of recognizing the abilities of children with dyslexia, including examples of people with dyslexia who are at the very top of their career fields.

Copies of Dr. Sally Shaywitz' award-winning, best-selling book, Overcoming Dyslexia, will be available for purchase, and she will sign copies after the presentation.

Registration fee is $20
