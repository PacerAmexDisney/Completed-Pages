Simon Technology Center workshops

Technology for Girls: STEM Club, Electrify

Saturday, Jan. 21, 2017 from 9 to 10:30 a.m.

To have your inventions working, you need something very important: electricity. This STEM Club session hosted by the Green Girls from the Eagan robotics team will focus on all things electrical. Middle school girls with disabilities will gain a general understanding of electricity. Participants will apply what they learn and create wacky, LED circuit creatures and potato clocks! Register online

Location: PACER Center

Technology for Girls: A Digital Game of Simon Says

Saturday, Feb. 4, 2017 from 9 to 10:30 a.m.

Middle school girls with disabilities are invited to join mentors from the University of Minnesota's MnDrive program to learn how fun programming can be. Participants will program lights, buzzers, and more following instructions based on code they write! No experience necessary. Register online

Location: PACER Center
