Action Alert: Call today! Help Oppose Healthcare Bill that will Jeopardize Services for Children with Disabilities

The U.S. House is expected to vote today on the American Health Care Act (AHCA), the bill to replace the Affordable Care Act (aka Obamacare). PACER is especially concerned about how proposed Medicaid changes will impact children with disabilities and their families. There is also talk the bill may repeal “essential health benefit” requirements, which would undermine existing protections such as coverage for pre-existing conditions, ban on lifetime limits, and coverage for mental health and substance abuse treatment.

If you live in a district with the following representatives (Rep. Paulsen — greater Hennepin County, Rep. Lewis — south metro, or Rep. Emmer — far west metro),  please consider contacting their office today and asking them to vote against the AHCA because of its negative impacts on individuals with disabilities and their families.
