Want to know the most recent information on PACER’s efforts to prevent bullying? The new PACER Bullying Prevention E-News debuts in April, and you can subscribe today to receive these monthly updates.

Bullying is pervasive in American schools but PACER’s National Bullying Prevention Center is making a difference by uniting, engaging, and educating communities nationwide to prevent bullying. With the support of teachers, parents, students, and celebrities, PACER’s creative, relevant, and interactive resources are making an impact.

We have a lot going on and you’ll want to stay informed.

Every month you’ll receive current information including:

  • Practical tools you can use to help prevent bullying where you live
  • Inspiring stories on students around the country who are taking action to prevent bullying
  • Breaking news about upcoming events like Run, Walk, Roll Against Bullying, Unity Day, and more
  • New resources available from PACER’s National Bullying Prevention Center
  • Notice on updates to the PACERTeensAgainstBullying.org and PACERKidsAgainstBullying.org websites
  • The latest on the work of our partner organizations such as thebullyproject.com
