We are pleased to announce that Lela Restaurant in Bloomington will team up with PACER’s National Bullying Prevention Center for November’s Cocktails for a Cause, a happy hour with a purpose. Chef Stewart Woodman and his team have invited Paula Goldberg to be the celebrity bartender for the kick-off event.

Wednesday, November 2
6 to 8 p.m.
Sheraton Bloomington Hotel (formerly the Sofitel), 5601 W. 78th St.

The November cocktail created to celebrate PACER’s National Bullying Prevention Center will be the “Fire in the Orchard” for $12, with $6 of the price going directly to PACER. The cocktail is made with Du Nord Apple, Smoked Minnesota Honey Crisp apple juice, baked apple bitters, Bulleit Bourbon, Cointreau, a slice of jalapeno, a sugar cube, and garnished with a Bourbon-soaked cinnamon stick. The cocktail will be available at Lela throughout November.

Please join us to support bullying prevention and watch Paula stir up some fun on Wednesday, November 2!
