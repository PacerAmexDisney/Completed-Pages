Support special education services by helping PACER tell success stories to Minnesota’s decision-makers

Dear Parents,

Recent news coverage has focused on problems with special education. We know that many children have benefited greatly from the special education services they have received in school. It’s important for Minnesota’s decision-makers to know how special education leads to success for so many children with disabilities. Please respond to this survey with your experiences.

We would love to hear your success stories to provide a more balanced view. If you are willing to share your story with others, please respond to the optional section on the last page of the survey.

Thank you for your participation!

Please complete one survey for each child with a disability.
