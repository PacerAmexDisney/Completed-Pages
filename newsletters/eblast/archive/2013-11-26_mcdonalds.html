Enjoy a Fridley McDonald’s Happy Meal ® By 12.31.13 and Help PACER Center Prevent Bullying!

The newly opened McDonalds in Fridley is donating 20 cents of every Happy Meal® sold through December 31, 2013, to PACER’s National Bullying Prevention Center.

This opportunity is available at the Fridley McDonalds at 244 57th Avenue NE, near the intersection of Highway 694 and University Avenue.

One of every three students is bullied—and it might be happening to a child that you know and care about. Your purchase of a Happy Meal ® supports the efforts of Minnesota-based PACER’s National Bullying Prevention Center. When you make a difference it can mean one less student being bullied, one more person speaking out, or one more child knowing that they are not alone.

Buy a Happy Meal ® at the Fridley McDonalds and show children that their community cares.

Sincerely,
