Parents of children with disabilities and professionals who work with them give their hearts to helping kids with disabilities, and PACER can offer support! If you or someone you know is looking for information, training, and support for a child or young adult with disabilities, please forward this e-mail and the suggestions below.

  • Call PACER at 952-838-9000 and speak to a parent advocate who can answer your questions
  • Visit PACER.org to find plenty of free resources
  • Sign up for PACER’s free newsletters
  • Send an e-mail to PACER@PACER.org
  • Attend a free workshop and learn more about opportunities for your child’s education and other services
  • Follow PACER on Facebook and Twitter
  • Visit PACER’s National Bullying Prevention Center and find ways to address bullying
