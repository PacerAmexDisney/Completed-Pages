Dear Parent,

The Minnesota Statewide Family Network and PACER Center are conducting a brief survey of Minnesota parents of children with emotional or behavioral needs ages birth to 21. We want to know what types of information and resources would be most helpful to you as we plan new trainings, materials, and other activities.

The survey will take approximately 5 minutes to complete. We’d appreciate your response by February 8, 2013. All responses are confidential, and individual data will not be shared with anyone.
