Don’t miss the “Dunn Goodie”
Coffee Break May 24!

Dunn Bros Coffee & Pearson’s Candy Team Up to Support
PACER’s National Bullying Prevention Center

Stop by any Twin Cities metro area Dunn Bros from 3-6 p.m. on Thursday, May 24 and you’ll receive a free Pearson’s Nut Goodie to go along with your beverage!

Dunn Bros will donate 50 cents from each beverage purchased to support PACER’s National Bullying Prevention Center – and you can make a larger contribution at the register!

*Wine, beer, and bottled beverages not included

 

It’s time to celebrate

  • The 100th anniversary of Pearson’s Nut Goodie
  • The 25th anniversary of Dunn Bros Coffee
  • The important work of PACER’s National Bullying Prevention Center

 

Bullying is pervasive in American schools but PACER’s National Bullying Prevention Center is making a difference by uniting, engaging, and educating communities nationwide to prevent bullying. Want to make a difference in your community?  Learn more
