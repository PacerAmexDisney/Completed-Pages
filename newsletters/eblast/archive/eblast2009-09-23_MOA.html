Special Live Event at the Mall of America— The Time to Take Action Against Bullying is Now!

When: Tuesday, Oct. 6, from 4:15 to 5 p.m.

Where: Mall of America’s Best Buy Rotunda (Level 1, East between Sears & Bloomingdales)

Teens, kids, parents and educators: Show your support for the movement against bullying at an exciting Mall of America (MOA) —Best Buy Rotunda —event on Tuesday, Oct. 6, from 4:15 to 5 p.m.

Please let us know if you can attend this important event! RSVP on-line or by calling 952-838-9000.

As part of PACER Center’s fourth annual National Bullying Prevention Awareness Week, Oct. 4 – 10, 2009, children, teens, adults, schools, and communities across the country are being encouraged to prevent bullying. You know bullying is never okay. So do something about it! Come to the Mall of America’s Best Buy Rotunda on Tuesday, Oct. 6 from 4:15 to 5 p.m. for a special event where you can join others to show you care about this important issue! (Level 1, East between Sears & Bloomingdales)

Top Ten Reasons to Attend this Event!

  1. Influence change! Sign the petition to support the movement against bullying. More names = a stronger voice against bullying = more influence to change the laws to provide greater protection to students.
  2. Live Music!
    • Lindsay Rush, 20 - year old singer/songwriter will perform her new single “Odd Girl Out,” co-written by “The Gyrlz”—Daughtry and Hillary Duff. The song is about being bullied and feeling like an outcast.
    • Trent Romens Trio will perform from 4:15p.m. to 4:30p.m.
  3. Great giveaways! The first 300 teenagers to sign the online petition at the event will receive a Best Buy $10 gift card. Computers will be available for petition signing. More great give aways and prizes include free bookmarks, T-shirts, and more.
  4. Support Teens Against Bullying! Sign up on Best Buy’s @15 Web site (at15.com) Earn “points” that you can share with Teens Against Bullying to earn up to $250,000 for PACER’s Bullying Prevention Center and TeensAgainstBullying.org.
  5. Amazing drawings!
    • Teens (13-18) who sign up on at15.com during the event may place their name in a drawing for an iPod touch!
    • Help out your school/organization. Anyone with 5 or more students attending can enter their school/organization in a drawing to receive one of ten $100 Best Buy gift cards.
  6. Guest speakers: Present on bullying prevention issues and the importance of starting a movment against bullying.
  7. Share why you care! You’ll have a chance to submit your thoughts to “I Care Because . . .” and post them on the PACERTeensAgainstBullying.org Web site
  8. Videos! Watch the newly released public service announcement by Disney star and teen music sensation Demi Lovato!
  9. Contests! Elementary school students can provide artwork to be posted to PACER’s Kids Against Bullying Web site.
  10. Meet the cast! Don’t miss the appearance by the Kids Against Bullying puppets!
