A free PACER workshop for parents

Bullying Prevention: What Parents Need to Know

Tuesday, Sept. 27, 2016
6:30 to 8:30 p.m.

Bullying can be an isolating experience, not just for the kids involved, but for their parents, too. Fortunately, there is help. This workshop will offer a comprehensive overview for parents to learn what they can do to address and prevent bullying.

Register Here

Topics include :

  • Definition and types of bullying
  • Ways to help your child
  • Students with disabilities
  • Working with the school
  • Laws and policy
  • Information on available resources
  • National Bullying Prevention Month

Attend online via Livestream!

To attend the workshop via Livestream, you must register online at PACER.org/workshops. You will receive the Livestream link in your confirmation e-mail.
