Dear Friends:

We have just learned some exciting new details about the Nordstrom Fall 2012 Designer Preview on June 21 at 6:30 p.m. An amazing new set design will deliver the genuine experience of a New York-style runway show right here in Minnesota, and you will be no more than one row from the action! Models from across America will fly in to produce this first-class fashion preview which will transform Nordstrom’s entire second floor. Cocktails, hors d’oeuvres, desserts and an elegant gift bag complete this very special evening which benefits PACER Center.

Tickets to this exclusive event are very limited and we anticipate a sell out!  – purchase your tickets now at www.pacer.org/nordstrom.

This unique runway design ensures an intimate setting where everyone has an up-close view of the couture.

Models from New York, Chicago, Dallas and Los Angeles will present this exclusive preview of iconic and emerging American and European Designers.

Thank you for joining me in supporting PACER Center, Champions for Children.
