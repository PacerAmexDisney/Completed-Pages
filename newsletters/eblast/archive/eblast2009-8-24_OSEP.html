Dear Parents,

We need your help so we can provide information to the U.S. Department of Education's Office of Special Education Programs (OSEP) as to how we think children from birth to age 3 are being served in the State of Minnesota. 

The Office of Special Education Programs (OSEP)in Washington, DC is visiting Minnesota's Department of Education (MDE) for a verification visit in the next couple of weeks to learn how the Individuals with Disabilities Education Act (IDEA) is working for Minnesota families and their children.  Please take a moment to take this brief survey which will provide valuable feedback for OSEP.

Responses are confidential, names will not be used and results will be presented in summary form.  We look forward to hearing from you and thank you for your time!  Please return to us by August 31, 2009. If you have questions, please feel free to call us at 952-838-9000 or toll free at 888.248.0822.

Sincerely,

 

Paula Goldberg
PACER Center's Executive Director
