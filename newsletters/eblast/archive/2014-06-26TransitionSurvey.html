PACER Center is excited to launch a new National Parent Center on Transition and Employment website to inspire possibilities for students with disabilities and their parents for life after high school. Topics for the website may include postsecondary education, employment, and life in the community.

You can be a partner in the website development process by providing valuable input on the most significant information needs your family has related to your youth’s transition from high school. PACER appreciates your willingness to complete this brief survey and values your input.
