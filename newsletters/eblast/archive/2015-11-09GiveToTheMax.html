On Thursday, November 12, you can be a Champion for Children by supporting PACER Center on "Give to the Max" Day. By making a tax-deductible donation, you will help PACER provide innovative programs for children with disabilities and those who are bullied.

At PACER Center we are fortunate to see the results of your giving every day through the children and families we are able to help. When you support PACER Center and PACER's National Bulling Prevention Center, you help ensure that we are here for:

  • One in every three students who are bullied.
  • Parents of children with disabilities and mental health issues such as depression and anxiety who are seeking information and assistance about services at school and in the community. 
  • Families of youth with disabilities who want to ensure a bright future for their children through education, employment, and independent living after high school.

With your support, we can continue to expand our important services for parents of children with disabilities and our programs that teach all children and young adults about bullying prevention.
