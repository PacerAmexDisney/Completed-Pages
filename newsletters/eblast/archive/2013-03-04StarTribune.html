How do you count the cost of special education in Minnesota?
Let your voice be heard!

After reading the March 3 Star Tribune article titled "Rising special ed cases are huge cost to Minnesota schools," PACER encourages parents of children with disabilities to share their views on the costs of special education and what special education services mean for their children as well as society as a whole.

How have special education services made a difference for your child? Please share your stories with the Star Tribune so its readers know the true impact of special education in the lives of students. Share your stories with us, too, by sending them to PACER@PACER.org.
