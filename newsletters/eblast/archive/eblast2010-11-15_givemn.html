Tuesday Nov. 16 is “Give to the Max Day”! Donate to PACER Today!

What if you could help us win $1,000 just by giving 10 bucks?  On Nov. 16, Give to the Max Day, you can.

Tuesday, Nov. 16 is “Give to the Max Day,” and donations made to PACER Center at http://givemn.razoo.com/story/Pacer-Center or http://givemn.razoo.com/story/National-Center-For-Bullying-Prevention will support PACER in its mission to help families of all children, including those with disabilities. Please contribute generously and make a difference in the life of a child!

GiveMN’s Give to the Max Day amplifies the impact of your donation in a number of ways:

  • Win a Golden Ticket! $1,000 will be given to a random donor’s charity every hour.  You could be that donor!  You could make several donations throughout the day and increase the odds!
  • Put us on the Leader Board!  Your donation on Nov. 16, could put us on the leader board to win $10,000 or even $20,000 for the most number of donors for our nonprofit. Help us get “on the board!”

The process is simple; the rewards are great:

  • Go to http://givemn.razoo.com/story/Pacer-Center or http://givemn.razoo.com/story/National-Center-For-Bullying-Prevention anytime Tuesday, Nov. 16 before midnight.
  • Make your online donation quickly, easily, and securely via credit card.
  • Know that you have made a difference in the lives of children with disabilities.

GiveMN.org is the smart way for you to discover, support, and engage with the charities that are right for you. The online giving platform allows you to find and support organizations that match your philanthropic goals and easily keep track of your donations all in a single online location.  Your information is private and secure; it will not be shared with anyone.

As you consider your charitable end-of-year giving, please consider participating in Give to the Max Day Nov. 16.  GiveMN is easy to use, and your gift to PACER will make a huge difference to our ongoing work.
