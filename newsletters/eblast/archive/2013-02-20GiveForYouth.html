PACER needs your vote in the Give for Youth Challenge

The Give for Youth Challenge is a crowd-funding opportunity supported by Microsoft that will help PACER’s bullying prevention programs impact more students. It only takes a couple of minutes but casting your vote for the “UNITE AGAINST BULLYING” project will help PACER develop new tools that students can use to expand their bullying prevention efforts and grow participation in Unity Day.

  • Microsoft has teamed up with GOOD, a civic-minded community platform to find 20 amazing nonprofits to feature on GiveforYouth.org, a microgiving portal that helps organizations fund projects for young people.
  • The top 20 vote-getters in the Give for Youth Challenge will earn the right to crowd fund their projects on GiveforYouth.org.
  • Microsoft will match funds raised up to $100,000!
  • Voting Ends March 1st!

VOTE NOW by clicking GiveForYouth.org > UniteAgainstBullying.

Please consider sharing this request
with others in your network so they can vote, too.

Thank you for your vote and your continued support of PACER!
