PACER Day at the Minnesota Capitol: “Be a Champion for Children!”

Wednesday, March 08, 2017, 9:30 a.m. to 1:00 p.m.

Where: State Office Building, Room 300S, 100 Rev. Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. Blvd. Saint Paul, MN 55155

Parents of children with disabilities have important stories to share that can impact systems change and improve outcomes for students. Participants will attend a dynamic workshop led by advocacy leaders and legislators to hear about potential legislation affecting special education, education funding, school discipline, and assistive technology.

Parents will learn how to effectively share their stories and enjoy a complimentary lunch before meeting with their state legislators.

Space is limited. Registration is required. Register online or call (952) 838-9000.
