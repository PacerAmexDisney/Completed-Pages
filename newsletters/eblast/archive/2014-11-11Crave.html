One out of every three students is bullied.
Chances are this could happen to someone you know and care about.
 

What can you do?

PACER’s National Bullying Prevention Center is making a difference for students, parents, and educators — and you can help. A portion of the proceeds from the CRAVE Chef’s Seasonal Menu will be donated to PACER’s National Bullying Prevention Center, and you can make a personal donation on your receipt. Your contribution can mean one less child being bullied, one less teen feeling alone, or one more person speaking out.

Visit Crave Online for more information.
PACER’s National Bullying Prevention Center actively leads social change, so that bullying is no longer considered an accepted childhood rite of passage.
