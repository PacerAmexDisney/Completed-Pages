Nov. 17 is “Give to the Max Day”! Donate to PACER Today and Your Gift Will Earn a Match

Tuesday, Nov. 17, is “Give to the Max Day,” and donations given to PACER Center at http://givemn.razoo.com/story/Pacer-Center will earn a portion of a $500,000* match. On that important day, your generous gift becomes magnified and you support PACER in its mission to provide free individual assistance, workshops, publications, and other resources to families of children with disabilities. Please contribute generously and make a difference in the life of a child.

Every dollar you donate (up to $2,500) earns the match and goes directly to PACER; there are no transaction fees! Itâ€™s all thanks to GiveMN.org, a new online giving tool for Minnesota nonprofits. As an added plus, the three nonprofits that receive donations from the most individual donors will receive additional cash prizes—so your donation can go even further.

On “Give to the Max Day,” we hope you will think of PACER Center and take advantage of this online matching gift program.

The process is simple; the rewards are great:

  • Go to http://givemn.razoo.com/story/Pacer-Center anytime between 8 a.m. Tuesday, Nov. 17 and 8 a.m. Wednesday, Nov. 18.
  • Make your online donation quickly, easily, and securely via credit card.
  • Know that you have made a difference in the lives of children with disabilities.

GiveMN.org is the smart way for you to discover, support, and engage with the charities that are right for you. The online giving platform allows you to find and support organizations that match your philanthropic goals and easily keep track of your donations all in a single online location.  Your information is private and secure; it will not be shared with anyone.

  • Give local. Find your perfect Minnesotan match, such as PACER Center.
  • Give fast. Make online donations quickly and easily via credit card to any U.S. nonprofit.
  • Give smart. Record all online contributions and receipts.

* Every donation made during Give to the Max Day will receive a portion of $500,000, pooled by several big Minnesota funders to match donations. The exact amount matched per dollar donated will be determined after Give to the Max Day concludes.

Individual donation transactions greater than $2,500 will be eligible for matching gifts up to $2,500 only. For example, if a donor made a $2,500 donation to an organization, the full $2,500 would be eligible to receive a portion of the matching funds. If instead she donated $10,000 to an organization, only $2,500 would be eligible to receive a portion of the matching dollars.

Examples of how the matching dollars will work: if Give to the Max Day inspires $500,000 in donations, we will provide $1 in matching funds per dollar donated to an organization; if Give to the Max Day inspires $2,000,000 in donations, we will provide $.25 in matching funds per dollar donated to an organization.
