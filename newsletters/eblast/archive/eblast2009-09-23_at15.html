Do You Know Any Teens? PACER Needs Their Help, Please Forward!

Teens’ "Votes" Can Help the Teens Against Bullying project at PACER be awarded up to $250,000!

Best Buys’ @15 Change Exchange program has chosen PACER as one of four organizations to receive a charitable donation. Teens’ votes determine what percentage of $250,000 PACER will receive to support its National Bullying Prevention Center and its new Web site, PACERTeensAgainstBullying.org.

What can teens, ages 13-18, do to make a difference and show they care about preventing bullying? Two quick and easy steps!

  1. Register on @15>>>
    • Receive 1500 points just for registering!
    • Get more points to spend by logging back in and doing activities.
    • Points are important! Every point influences a significant amount of money!
    • Refer a friend and receive an extra 150 points to share. Plus, the teen referring the most friends will receive an 8GB iPod Touch. Runner up will receive a $50 Best Buy card.
  2. Spend the points>>>
    • A password will be sent to the member’s email account.
    • Log back in with user name and password (upper right corner) on at15.com
    • Give your points—up to Oct. 30 — to TEENS AGAINST BULLYING!

When teens vote for PACER, they:

  • Empower teens everywhere to stand up to bullying
  • Build the teens against bullying movement
  • Influence a new way of thinking about an age old issue
  • Make their voice heard round the world

Tell teens you know to sign up now and pass the word!
