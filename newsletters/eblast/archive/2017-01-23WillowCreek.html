Exciting Movie and Cocktail Party - March 9, 2017 at the new Willow Creek Theater
view online
   

Special opportunity for a limited number of people.

Order Your Tickets Today

Join PACER on Thursday, March 9 for the grand opening of the newly remodeled Willow Creek Theater located near General Mills (9900 Shelard Pkwy., Plymouth, MN 55441). The new owner, Emagine Entertainment, has chosen PACER Center as its nonprofit partner for this exciting event, which means all proceeds from ticket sales will support PACER.

Tickets ($100 each) include a pre-movie cocktail party at the theater with food, wine, and valet parking. Then enjoy one of the new feature films offered at the theater.

The cocktail party will take place from 6-8 p.m. with the movies beginning at 8 p.m. Several movies will be available for viewing.

These include:

  • The Great Wall starring Matt Damon. A man is imprisoned within the Great Wall and discovers the mystery behind one of the greatest wonders of the world.

  • Logan starring Hugh Jackman and Patrick Stewart. Part of the X-Men series, Logan’s attempts to hide his legacy and Professor X from the world.

  • Kong: Skull Island starring Tom Hiddleston, Samuel L. Jackson, and John Goodman. Explorers encounter a gigantic ape and other creatures after traveling to an island in the Pacific Ocean (see this movie a day before it’s released to the public!).

    (while not expected, movies are subject to change)

Tickets can be purchased at www.pacer.org/willowcreek or by calling 952-838-9000.
