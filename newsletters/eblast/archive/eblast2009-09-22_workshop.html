Understanding Schoolwide
Positive Behavioral Interventions and Supports Workshop

What Parents and Professionals Need to Know

A positive school environment helps all students do better academically.
Schoolwide Positive Behavioral Interventions and Supports (SW-PBIS) can
help schools achieve that positive school climateâ€”especially when parents are
involved.

This workshop will help parents and professionals understand the concept
of SW-PBIS and its application for all students.

Participants will:
  • Build a common understanding about SW-PBIS
  • Learn effective alternatives to punishment
  • Reinforce positive behavioral skills in children
  • Share common strengths, successes, and frustrations
  • Discover how schools are implementing SW-PBIS
