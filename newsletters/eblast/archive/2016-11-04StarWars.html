A Special Opportunity to See the New STAR WARS “Rogue One: A Star Wars Story” Before it’s Released to the Public!

Grand Opening Event and Cocktail Party at the newly remodeled Willow Creek Theater in Plymouth on Thursday, December 15th

The new owner, Emagine Entertainment, has chosen PACER as their non-profit partner for this exciting event which means all proceeds from ticket sales will come to PACER Center.

Tickets ($100 each) include a pre-movie cocktail party at the theater with food, beer, wine, and valet parking before the amazing opportunity to see the new Star Wars movie “Rogue One: A Star Wars Story” a day before it opens to the public!

The cocktail party will take place from 6-8 PM. The first showing of Star Wars will begin at 8 PM, with a second showing beginning at 8:30 PM. Other new movies may also be playing on one or two screens.
