Dear Friends,

We hope you will join us March 9th for the grand opening of the newly remodeled Willow Creek theater and support PACER in the process! Attend a cocktail party before watching one of the Oscar nominated films.

  • Enjoy valet service, walk the red carpet with photographers taking your picture as you arrive at the event beginning at 6 p.m.
  • Complimentary open bar, heavy hors d'oeuvres and a gourmet popcorn station.
  • At 8 p.m. move into one of the theaters to enjoy the luxurious reclining chairs while watching one of several Oscar-nominated movies.

    • Oscar nominated titles include: Manchester by the Sea, La La Land, Hidden Figures, Fences or Lion
    • If new releases are more your interest, see The Shack, A United Kingdom, Lego Batman, Logan, Kong
  • Best of All — 100% of proceeds from ticket sales for this Grand Opening Event will come to PACER Center!

    • $75 — Individual Tickets
    • $50 — Group Tickets (available with purchase of 4 or more tickets.)
  • Tickets can be purchased at www.pacer.org or by calling PACER at 952.838.9000

Location: Newly remodeled Willow Creek theater
Address: 9900 Shelard Parkway, Plymouth, MN 55441 (near General Mills)
Date: March 9, 2017
