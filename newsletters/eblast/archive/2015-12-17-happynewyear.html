This year‘s PACER holiday card combines the artwork of two talented young artists, Alexa and Nathalie. They participated in PACER‘s Annual Creative Kids Contest for children with disabilities.

Alexa is a creative 13-year-old who enjoys expressing herself through drawing, painting, animation, and art classes. This past year, she won first prize for her art at both the Dakota County Art Fair and at the Minnesota State Fair. She also loves science and the study of the earth. She embraces her ADHD, anxiety and sensory processing disorders as a gift she was given to share with others. Alexa aspires to continue using her art to make a difference in other people‘s lives.

Nathalie Rieder is a 7th grader at Minnetonka Middle School East in Minnesota. She loves singing, making jewelry, volunteering at her church nursery, and being with her dog Bruce.
