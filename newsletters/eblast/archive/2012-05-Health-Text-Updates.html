NEW – Health Information Alerts from PACER's Family-To-Family Health Information Center via text message

People can sign up to receive free updates via text message! This new PACER service provides families  and others with specific information from PACER programs including notice of upcoming workshops, reminders of upcoming medical assistance deadlines, tips, and new PACER resources. Your plan’s text messaging rates will apply.

For updates from PACER’s Family to Family Health Information Center, text “Health” to 27138.
