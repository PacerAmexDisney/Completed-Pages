PACER Makes it Easy to Give a Car, Help a Child

Selling your used vehicle can be a hassle. Donating your car to PACER’s Give a Car, Help a Child program is easy. By donating your car or other vehicle to PACER, you will help support children with disabilities and children who are bullied. You’ll also receive a tax deduction.

Vehicles should be 1991 or newer and running. Exceptions can be made depending on the vehicle’s condition. Donating your vehicle to PACER is easy. Simply gather your keys, vehicle title, and lien release, if applicable. Then contact PACER to discuss how to donate your vehicle. Once the vehicle has been sold, PACER will provide you with a written acknowledgement of the tax deductible value of your donation. Please be sure to keep the acknowledgement letter with your other tax records.

By donating a vehicle to PACER, you can make a difference in the lives of all children, including those with disabilities. For more information on the Give a Car, Help a Child program, please call Jackie Saffert at PACER at (952) 838-9000 or e-mail jackie.saffert@pacer.org.
