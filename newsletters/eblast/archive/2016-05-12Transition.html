Dear Parents and Guardians,

We want your help to improve transition services for youth with disabilities in Minnesota! If you have a child (ages 16-21) who attends public school in Minnesota and has an Individualized Education Program (IEP), please take a few minutes to complete this survey.

PACER and the Minnesota Department of Education (MDE) would like your honest feedback on how the secondary transition process is working to support your youth with options for competitive, integrated employment and postsecondary education after they exit high school. The data gathered from this survey will be shared with the MDE so they may improve activities and better support youth and families. All responses are anonymous, and no identifying information will be shared.

Thank you in advance for taking the time to complete this survey. If you have questions, please contact Sean Roy at PACER Center - sroy@pacer.org.
