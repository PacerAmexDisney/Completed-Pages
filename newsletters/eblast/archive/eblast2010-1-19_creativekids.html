PACER’s Creative Kids Contest

Join PACER, other families, and celebrated artist Tony Whelihan on Saturday, February 27th for this fun family event where imaginations run wild. Children spend the morning expressing themselves and their creative ideas through various mediums and vibrant colors.

Their masterpieces are then entered in PACER’s Annual Creative Kids Contest and the winner’s artwork will become the cover design of the 2010 PACER Greeting Card, which will be sold online and at the PACER Benefit. The original artwork of the top 10 designs will be sold at PACER’s Benefit Silent Auction. More Info >>>

To register or to donate art supplies, e-mail Nicole Stanich at nicole.stanich@pacer.org or call 952-838-9000.
Space is limited.

Who’s Invited: Creative Kids with Disabilities and their Families

When: Saturday, February 27, 2010 from 10am-12pm

Where: Bloomington Art Center, 1800 W Old Shakopee Road, Bloomington, MN
For directions, visit www.bloomingtonartcenter.com/Pages/Location.htm.
