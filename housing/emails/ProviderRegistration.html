Registration Deadline: Monday, March 20th

Please join us for this exciting opportunity to connect with families who are eager to find out what housing resources your agency/company has to offer.

PACER's Housing Resource and Information Fair will partner parents and providers and support organizations in a unique setting that will include a keynote speaker, exhibitors, and question and answer rooms. The continuing goal of PACER's Housing Program is to develop information and resources to help parents of young adults with disabilities understand their options for independent living and housing.

We will only be able to accommodate a limited number of providers and support organizations. PACER reserves the right to select providers based on disability type and population served. Availability for those chosen is on a first come first serve basis.

HOUSING FAIR INFORMATION

  • Saturday, April 15th at PACER Center
  • Table assignment and set up is at 8:00 a.m.
  • Information tables will be up until 12:00 p.m.
  • Table size is 18"x 60"
  • Wireless internet available. Limited power cords available.

REGISTRATION/FEES
