Coming in Spring 2014!

PACER’s 10th Annual Housing Resource and Information Fair
Saturday, April 12, 2014 at PACER Center

PACER’s housing fair is moving to Spring 2014, and will be held from 9 a.m. to noon on Saturday, April 12. It is an excellent opportunity for families of children and young adults to meet with service providers and discuss housing options, services, supports, and related issues for children and young adults with disabilities. We look forward to your participation.

To learn more, e-mail Susan Shimota at susan.shimota@pacer.org or call (952) 838-9000.
