PACER’s Creative Kids Event and Contest

Children with disabilities and their families are invited to join PACER for a morning of creativity, fun, and exploration around the themes of unity, inclusion, kindness and friendship.

Kids will use their imaginations to express themselves through various mediums and vibrant colors. The artwork of children with disabilities will be entered into the Creative Kids contest. The winner will become the cover design of the PACER Greeting Card, which is sold online and at the Benefit. The original artwork of the top 8 designs will be sold at PACER’s Benefit Silent Auction.

Register Your Kids Today

Space is limited; advance registration is required. An adult must stay with the child during the event.

Who’s Invited: All kids are welcome to create artwork. However, Creative Kids with disabilities ages 4 to 17 are eligible for the contest.

When: Saturday, February 25, 2017 from 10 a.m. to 12 p.m.

Where: PACER Center, 8161 Normandale Blvd. Bloomington, MN 55437
