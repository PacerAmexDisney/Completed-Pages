Dear Parents,

We hope you will join PACER Center for the 11th Annual Symposium about Children and Young Adults with Mental Health and Learning Disabilities taking place on Tuesday, August 9. The Symposium will be held at the Minneapolis Convention Center from 8 AM — 4 PM. The day is filled with 3 keynote speakers — Dr. Read Sulik, BraVada Garrett-Akinsanya, Ph.D., and Adam Levy — as well as breakout workshop sessions on dyslexia, medications, concussions and more. The cost to attend is $30 which includes lunch and CEU’s.

To register or to find out more information on the Symposium, speakers, and workshop topics, please visit: pacer.org/symposium
