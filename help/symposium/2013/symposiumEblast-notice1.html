Dear Registrant,

We are so pleased that you will be joining us for the 8th Annual National PACER Symposium on Tuesday, August 6th, 8:00 a.m. at the Minneapolis Convention Center.  It is located at 1301 2nd Ave. South, Minneapolis, Minn. For freeway directions to MCC as well as nearby parking see the map link to the rigtht.

The keynote speakers are listed below.  Please note that the presentation by Dr. John Ratey is based on his book “Spark:  The Revolutionary New Science of Exercise and the Brain.”  If you would like to read it prior to the symposium, it is available in paperback through Barnes & Noble or Amazon.

Please let us know if you need to cancel by calling (952) 838-9000, as we have others interested in attending.

(Download the Agenda)

Keynote Speakers:

  • Ken Barlow, Meteorologist at KSTP-TV
  • John Maag, Ph.D., University of Nebraska-Lincoln Professor specializing in the treatment of adolescents with emotional and behavioral disorders
  • John Ratey, M.D., Associate Clinical Professor of Psychiatry at Harvard Medical School

Workshop Topics:

  • Controlling Your Emotions and Behavior Regardless of How Disagreeably Others Treat You
  • Scared, Sad, and Spacey:  Handling Emotional Distress in the Classroom
  • The Use of Prone Restraint in School:  Minnesota’s Experience
  • Siblings Forever:  Brothers and Sisters Share Their Stories
  • Important Lessons Learned in Parenting a Child with Disabilities
  • Student Success for $100 or Less:  Free and Low-cost Assistive Technology for Students
  • From Naughty to Nice:  The Road to Positive Behavior Interventions

We look forward to seeing you on August 6th!
