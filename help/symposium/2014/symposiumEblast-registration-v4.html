Ninth Annual National PACER Symposium

About Children & Young Adults with Mental Health & Learning Disabilities
Join L. Read Sulik, MD, a child, adolescent and adult psychiatrist and a pediatrician, as he presents two workshops at the Symposium: “Understanding the Enormity of Depression and How to Develop Skills Needed for Healing in Children and Young Adults” and “Anxiety from the Inside Out”. Dr. Sulik’s workshops are highly interactive with real-life examples and stories to help participants better understand depression and anxiety in children and young adults.
