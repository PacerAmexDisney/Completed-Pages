Dear Registrant,

(Download the Agenda)

The countdown is on! The 9th Annual National PACER Symposium is Thursday, August 7th, at the Minneapolis Convention Center.

For freeway directions to MCC as well as nearby parking click here.

Registration begins at 8:00 a.m., with opening remarks at 8:30. The symposium ends at 4:30 p.m., at which time you’ll receive your Certificate of Attendance for 6 clock hours. Certificates will not be available until the end of the day.

Check out samples of PACER’s resource information at the PACER Materials table. There will be a limited number of booklets for sale. Please note that all purchases are cash or check only.

Some things to keep in mind:

  • Your name badge is required for admittance to the ballroom and workshop sessions.
  • Print your resources ahead of time! For those who would like printed copies of the session materials, please: Download the materials here.
  • Box lunches will be provided, however, breakfast is not included, so please don’t come hungry!
  • Bring a sweater; meeting rooms can be chilly.

Come ready for an exciting day of keynote presentations and interactive workshop sessions!
