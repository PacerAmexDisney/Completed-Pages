Dear Registrant,

We are so pleased that you will be joining us for the 9th Annual National PACER Symposium on Thursday, August 7th, at the Minneapolis Convention Center.  It is located at 1301 2nd Avenue South, Minneapolis, Minn. For freeway directions to MCC as well as nearby parking see the map link to the right.

The keynote speakers as well as workshop topics are listed below.

Please let us know if you need to cancel by calling (952) 838-9000, as we have others interested in attending.

(Download the Agenda)

Keynote Speakers:

  • Dr. Susan Jenkins, Director of Bluestem Center for Child and Family Development
  • Jo Mascorro, education consultant specializing in behavior, communication, and programming for students with disabilities
  • Larry Wexler, Ph.D., Division Director for the Research to Practice Division of the U.S. Department of Education, Office of Special Education Programs (OSEP)

Workshop Topics:

  • Understanding the Enormity of Depression and How to Develop Skills for Healing in Children and Young Adults
  • Anxiety from the Inside Out
  • What Do We Mean When We Talk About Aspergers?
  • Restraint, Seclusion, and Exclusion: An Informal Discussion
  • What it Takes: Effectively Supporting Young Children with the Most Challenging Behaviors
  • Transition to Employment: Two Sides of the Same Coin
  • Navigating School Discipline and Juvenile Justice Issues for Children with Disabilities
  • Student Success for $100 or Less:  Free and Low-cost Assistive Technology for Students with Learning Disabilities
  • Working with Parents, Teachers and Others: A Team Approach to Meeting the Needs of Students with Challenging Behaviors that Interfere with Teaching and Learning

We look forward to seeing you on August 7th!
