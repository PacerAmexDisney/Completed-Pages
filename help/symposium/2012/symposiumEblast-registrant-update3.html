Dear Registrant,

The countdown is on! There are only a few more days until the 7th Annual National Ted & Dr. Roberta Mann Foundation Symposium on Monday, August 6th, at the Doubletree by Hilton Bloomington-Minneapolis South Hotel, located at 7800 Normandale Boulevard. There is free parking at the hotel. Registration begins at 8:00 a.m., with the opening remarks at 8:30 a.m. The symposium ends at 4:30 p.m., at which time you’ll receive your Certificate of Attendance for 6 clock hours. Certificates will not be available until the end of the day.

In a “green” initiative, we will not be providing copies of presentations in your symposium folder. For those of you who would like to print your own copies, please go to the Mann Symposium page on PACER’s website under the Symposium Presentation Handouts and Resources section. http://www.pacer.org/help/symposium/

A limited number of DVDs by Dan Habib, and Rebecca Banks’ and Diane Kennedy’s book Bright Not Broken: Gifted Kids, ADHD, and Autism will be available for purchase, as well as some PACER materials.
Please note: All purchases are cash or check only.

Some other things to keep in mind:

  • Your name badge is required for admittance to the ballroom and workshop sessions.
  • Boxed lunches will be provided, however, breakfast is not included, so please don’t come hungry!
  • Bring a sweater; meeting rooms can be chilly.
  • All seats are non-refundable, but we’d appreciate it if you could let us know if you need to cancel by replying to this email or calling (952) 838-9000.

Come ready for an exciting day of keynote presentations and interactive workshop sessions! See you soon!
