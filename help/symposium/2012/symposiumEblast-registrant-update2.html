Dear Participant,

Thank you for registering for the 7th Annual National Ted & Dr. Roberta Mann Foundation Symposium on Monday, August 6th, at the Doubletree by Hilton Bloomington-Minneapolis South Hotel, located at 7800 Normandale Boulevard. There is free parking at the hotel. Registration begins at 8:00 a.m., with the opening remarks at 8:30 a.m.

In a “green” initiative, we will not be providing copies of presentations in your symposium folder. For those of you who would like to print your own copies, please go to the Mann Symposium page on PACER’s website under the Symposium Presentation Handouts and Resources section.

http://www.pacer.org/help/symposium/

The workshop session presented by Rebecca Banks and Diane Kennedy will reference the book “Bright Not Broken: Gifted Kids, ADHD, and Autism.” It’s available through Barnes & Noble or Amazon if you’d like to read it prior to the symposium. A limited number of books will be available for purchase on-site. Additionally, Dan Habib’s DVDs “Including Samuel”and “Who Cares About Kelsey?” will also be sold at the symposium. Please note: All purchases are cash or check only.

The event ends at 4:30 p.m., at which time you’ll receive your Certificate of Attendance for 6 clock hours. Certificates will not be available until the end of the day.

We are completely sold out! All seats are non-refundable, but we’d appreciate it if you could let us know if you need to cancel by replying to this email or calling (952) 838-9000.

We look forward to an exciting day on August 6th!
