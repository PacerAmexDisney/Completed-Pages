Private Insurance
Author: PACER Center
There are numerous private insurance options for both
individuals and families in Minnesota. Private insurance can
come through an employer or be purchased individually.
Some employers automatically take a certain amount from
employees' pay for health care coverage or simply include
health coverage as a benefit. At other workplaces, employees
pay a monthly premium.
