Tax

Internal Revenue Service (IRS)

Call the IRS with any tax-related question, or use its Web site to search on any tax issue or name of an IRS publication: www.irs.gov; 1-800-829-1040.

Volunteer Income Tax Assistance (VITA)

VITA is a government program that provides free tax preparation services for people with certain levels of income. To find out what income level is required to qualify for VITA services and if there is a VITA site located near you, Visit www.irs.gov and search on “VITA”;   1-800-906-9887.
