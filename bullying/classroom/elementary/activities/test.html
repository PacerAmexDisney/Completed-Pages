Celebrating Acceptance and Inclusion Through Teamwork

Digital Teacher Toolkit

You are invited to register for the resources of the free “T.E.A.M. Rudolph and the Reindeer Games” Digital Teacher Toolkit so that you and all of your students can join T.E.A.M. Rudolph and celebrate the differences in us all!

Register to Acess the Free Online T.E.A.M. Rudolph Toolkit

and Discussion Guide		 Streaming
Rudolph Music

Many know the classic, timeless story of how Rudolph the Red-Nosed Reindeer was laughed at by his peers and excluded from the Reindeer Games for being different. But do you know what became of the Reindeer Games?

“T.E.A.M. Rudolph and the Reindeer Games” reveals the untold story of how the Reindeer Games became a celebration of acceptance and inclusion.

“We’ll all join T.E.A.M. Rudolph!”
Santa said, filled with pride.
“We’ll all work together.
We’ll all play on one side.
We’ll Treat Everyone As Members.
Treat everyone as friends.
Treat everyone with kindness that just never ends.“

The toolkit provides access to online streaming of a video picture book and Rudolph music, and downloadable classroom learning activities with discussion questions and guidelines based on the new book.

Purchase a print copy of T.E.A.M. RUDOPLH and a portion of the proceeds will be donated to PACER’s National Bullying Prevention Center.

Classroom activities created by Julia Magnasco, Education Director, First Stage In cooperation with PACER’s National Bullying Prevention Center
