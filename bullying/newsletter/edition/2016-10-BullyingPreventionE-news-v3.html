Teen developer of SIT WITH US app partners with PACER’s NBPC

At 16 years old, Natalie designed the Sit With Us app to address bullying by helping students who have difficulty finding a place to sit and also locate a welcoming group in the lunchroom. Natalie is now partnering with PACER’s National Bullying Prevention Center to reach more teens to create inclusive communities. Learn More

Cast and crew of Loserville celebrate Unity Day

The cast and crew of @LoservilleFilm created four new videos for PACER’s National Bullying Prevention Center. The video titled “We've Been There, Know You're Not Alone” sends the important message to know that if you are being bullied, there are people who care and want to help. Cast member Marco James Marguez shares, “I know what it’s like to feel like you’re alone—when you feel like that, it’s the best time to reach out to people and ask for help.”

Share your story

Morgan writes, "I was bullied in elementary school because I was short and different. Kids would tease me, call me names, and spread rumors about me. I started to come home from school crying and sometimes I would be crying when my parents dropped me off at school. So, I told my parents what was going on, they did something about it, and things got better for me. I want people to know that they're not alone and that things will get better and that they should never keep bullying a secret. No one deserves to be bullied.” Read more stories
