“Run, Walk, Roll Against Bullying” on Saturday, Oct. 1 in Bloomington and join the cause!

Join WCCO-TV’s Frank Vascellaro and Amelia Santaniello at the event

WHAT:  PACER’s National Bullying Prevention Center is inviting the Minnesota community to the second annual “Run, Walk, Roll Against Bullying,” a four-mile run or 1.5-mile fun walk/roll around beautiful Mt. Normandale Lake as part of PACER’s National Bullying Prevention Month of October.

REGISTER here or call 952-838-9000 and be a Champion for Children!

WHEN: Saturday, Oct. 1. Registration and race packet pick-up begin at 9 a.m., followed by run/walk/roll at 10 a.m.

WHERE: Mt. Normandale Lake, Normandale Blvd. and 84th St., Bloomington, Minn. 

COST: $20 per person. Each participant will receive a complimentary T-shirt. All proceeds from PACER’s Run, Walk, Roll Against Bullying will go to PACER's National Bullying Prevention Center. Additional donations welcome.

FUN & FREE REFRESHMENTS: at the Normandale Lake Bandshell immediately following the race!

  • WCCO-TV news anchors Frank Vascellaro and Amelia Santaniello will emcee.
  • Appearance by Robert Kroupa, author of “Just Like You,” a story about acceptance.
  • Performance by 13-year-old Hayley Reardon of an original song titled “Stand Together.”
  • Demonstration of the “Unity Dance,” which groups are encouraged to do on Unity Dance Day on Wednesday, Oct. 26, as part of National Bullying Prevention Month.
