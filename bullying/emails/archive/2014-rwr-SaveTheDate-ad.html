Save the Date! Saturday, September 20, 2014

Friends of PACER’s

Run, Walk, Roll Against Bullying

Help make a difference! Join PACER to support bullying prevention in Minnesota communities and to help raise funds for PACER’s National Bullying Prevention Center.

What: 

  • 4 mile run or
  • 2 mile walk/roll

When:

  • Saturday, September 20th
  • 9 a.m. - Registration and packet pick-up at Normandale Lake Bandshell
  • 10 a.m. - Run, Walk, Roll
  • 11 a.m. - Presentation speakers, prizes, and music

Where:

  • Mt. Normandale Lake Park (map)
  • Normandale Blvd. and 84th St., Bloomington

Registration Fee: (includes t-shirt)

  • $20.00: 17 years of age and older
  • $10.00: 7 – 16 years of age (Must be accompanied by an adult)
  • Free: 6 years of age and under (Must be accompanied by an adult)

 

Invite family and friends to this fun event; please consider pledging as well.  We hope many businesses and their employees will join the Run, Walk, Roll.
