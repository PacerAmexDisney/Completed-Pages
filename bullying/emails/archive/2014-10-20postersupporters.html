THANK YOU FOR SUPPORTING UNITY DAY 2014!

Thank you for ordering your 2014 UNITY DAY poster. You have joined thousands of schools, organizations and communities around the world in uniting against bullying!

Along with prominently displaying your poster, we want to encourage you to go orange on UNITY DAY, Oct. 22. Whether you wear an orange shirt, orange pair of shoes or orange sunglass, you’ll send a large message of support, hope and unity to students who are bullied. Other ways to celebrate UNITY DAY include holding a community event, selling orange cookies, releasing orange balloons, changing your Facebook cover photo to the UNITY DAY banner, or signing the digital petition – the possibilities are endless!

Make sure to let us know how you are celebrating UNITY DAY by posting to the UNITY DAY Facebook page, using the hashtag #UnityDay2014 on social media, and sending images and stories to bully411@pacer.org with permission to share. We can’t wait to see what you are doing to Make it Orange and Make it End!

Though the deadline for ordering a Unity Day shirt to be received by Oct. 22 has passed, you can still support the cause! Shirts are still available for purchase through the end of October.

Remember – when we stand together, no one has to stand alone! Thanks again for your support!

Sincerely,
