Disney’s Friends for Change & PACER to Hold
Bullying Prevention Rally at Mall of America

Unite Against Bullying Saturday, Oct. 27 at Noon

Featuring Free Live Performances by
Radio Disney’s “N.B.T.” Season Five Artists!

Disney’s Friends for Change, an initiative that inspires kids and families to take action, and the national nonprofit PACER will host a bullying prevention rally Oct. 27 to empower kids to unite against bullying in their schools and communities. The event – to be held on Make a Difference Day – will feature live, inspirational performances by Radio Disney’s “N.B.T” (Next Big Thing) season five artists, part of the national “N.B.T. On Tour.”

Where: The Rotunda at Mall of America
8100 24th Ave. S., Bloomington, Minn.

When: Saturday, Oct. 27, 2012
12 to 2 p.m. – Bullying Prevention Rally
2 to 3 p.m. – “N.B.T.” Artist Meet and Greet, Autograph Session

Who: Jake Whetter, Radio Disney on-air personality and “N.B.T.” host along with
“N.B.T.” Season Five Artists:

  • Sisters Chloe & Halle Bailey – 14- and 12-year-old pop singers and actresses
  • Katherine Hughes – 17-year-old acoustic pop singer and actress
  • Damian Otchere – 16-year-old pop and R&B singer
  • Jack Skuller – 16-year-old modern rock and pop singer, and songwriter
  • Elle Winter – 13-year-old pop singer, songwriter, and actress


For more event information e-mail Bullying411@PACER.org
