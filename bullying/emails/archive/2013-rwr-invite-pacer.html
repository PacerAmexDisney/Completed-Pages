We invite you to participate in PACER’s Run, Walk, Roll Against Bullying on Saturday, October 5. Every day 160,000 kids stay home from school for fear of being bullied; the effects can last a lifetime. Bullying.  Drama.   Harassment.   No matter what you call it, it hurts.

Fortunately, you can make a difference. 

Help by participating in the Friends of PACER’s Run, Walk, Roll Against Bullying on Saturday, Oct. 5, 2013 at Mt. Normandale Lake Park in Bloomington. This is a great opportunity to impact your community by showing that you care.   All proceeds go toward PACER’s National Bullying Prevention Center.

Check out our website: http://www.pacer.org/bullying/nbpm/runwalkroll/.  

Ask family and friends to sponsor you using the Pledge Form.

Want to Register as a Corporate Group?

  • If your company is interested in sponsoring and paying for a group of employees, complete the Group Registration.   Questions?  Contact Sarah Busch.
  • If employees are interested in registering and paying individually, please register now.

 

Thank you for your support!

Paula Goldberg, PACER Executive Director
