We invite you to participate in PACER’s Run, Walk, Roll Against Bullying on Saturday, September 20!

One in three students is bullied every year and the effects can last a lifetime. Bullying. Drama. Harassment. No matter what you call it, it hurts.

Fortunately, you can make a difference. 

Help by participating in the Friends of PACER’s Run, Walk, Roll Against Bullying on Saturday, September 20, 2014 at Mt. Normandale Lake Park in Bloomington. This is a great opportunity to impact your community by showing that you care. All proceeds go toward PACER’s National Bullying Prevention Center.

Register Today: http://www.pacer.org/bullying/nbpm/runwalkroll/.  

Ask family and friends to sponsor you using the Pledge Form.

 

Want to Register as a Corporate Group?

  • If your company is interested in sponsoring and paying for a group of employees, complete the Group Registration by contacting Madison Knapp.
  • If employees are interested in registering and paying individually, please contact Madison Knapp for a registration group code and they can register here.

 

Thank you for your support!

Paula Goldberg, PACER Executive Director
