Did you know that 1 in 3 students are bullied every year? The impact of bullying can last a lifetime – from depression and anxiety disorders to poor educational performance and emotional scars lasting into adulthood. PACER started National Bullying Prevention month in October of 2006. We appreciate your past support for PACER’s National Bullying Prevention Center, and we need your help now to make a difference for these children!

We hear from thousands of children and youth every year and they all need your help. The lonely teen who e-mails after midnight, unable to sleep because of the pain of being cyber-bullied day after day; the troubled parent who feels helpless trying to comfort his distraught child who has endured unspeakable mental and physical cruelty; the desperate teacher who is on the front lines every day and needs the right tools to deal with bullying in her school.

Last year, PACER’s National Bullying Prevention Center received visitors from nearly 200 countries on our bullying websites.  PACER is making a difference for these children, parents, and teachers across the country but we can’t do it without your help. Donate now if you believe every child deserves to feel safe at school.

Thank you for being a Champion for Children, both in the past and again now during National Bullying Prevention Month. We appreciate your support and your willingness to donate and make a difference in the lives of children!

“The end of bullying begins with you!”
