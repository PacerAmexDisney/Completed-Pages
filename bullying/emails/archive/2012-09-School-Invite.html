Every day in America, 160,000 kids stay home from school because they are afraid of being bullied. Fortunately you can help this Saturday, Oct. 6 and have fun doing it!

Register today for the Run, Walk, Roll Against Bullying at Mt. Normandale Lake Park in Bloomington. You, your students, and their families are invited to participate in this fun family event that raises awareness for bullying prevention. It is hosted by PACER’s National Bullying Prevention Center as part of its seventh annual National Bullying Prevention Month in October. This is a great opportunity to impact students, parents, teachers, and the community at large by showing how much you care.
