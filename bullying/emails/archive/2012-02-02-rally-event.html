Join the Bullying Prevention Movement with Students Demanding Change!
Rally at the State Capitol on Saturday, Feb. 11 from noon to 1 p.m.

Students: Tell your parents!!
Parents: Tell your kids!!

Join with the voices of Minnesota teens and make a statement to support stronger bullying prevention legislation. Did you know that Minnesota’s 37-word bullying prevention law is one of the weakest in America?

This is a student movement but parents, teachers, and concerned citizens are encouraged to join us as we stand up and protect each other.

“It’s not just about statistics. It’s about us as students,” said Audrey Berdahl-Baldwin, one of four Mounds Park Academy students leading Students Demanding Change with the support of PACER’s National Bullying Prevention Center. “We feel very strongly that every student has the right to go to school and feel safe, the right to be safe.”
