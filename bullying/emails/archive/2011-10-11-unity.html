UNITY DAY, Wednesday, Oct. 12 — Show your support during PACER's National Bullying Prevention Month

Let’s send a loud, UNITED, message of support to every student who has been bullied. Make it ORANGE and Make it End on Unity Day, Oct. 12!

What You Can Do

  1. “Attend” and “Share” the UNITY DAY events page on Facebook with your family, friends and school.
  2. Wear orange on Wednesday, Oct. 12. Post pictures to the UNITY DAY events page.
  3. Create a UNITY poster at your school and ask everyone to sign it.
  4. Find out more information at pacer.org/bullying/

Other Ways To Show Your Support

  1. Like PACER’s National Bullying Prevention Center Facebook page.
  2. Sign “The End of Bullying Begins With Me” online petition.
  3. Participate in PACER’s UNITY DANCE Day on Wednesday, Oct. 26, a day to dance and make a statement against bullying!

These events are part of PACER’s National Bullying Prevention Month of October. Throughout the month, organizations and individuals can show their support for the cause by visiting PACER.org/bullying. PACER’s National Bullying Prevention Center provides creative and interactive resources that are designed to benefit all students, including students with disabilities. It offers educators, students, families, and individuals the tools they need to address bullying in schools, recreational programs, or community organizations.
