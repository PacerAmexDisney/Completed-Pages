Every day in America, 160,000 kids stay home from school because they are afraid of being bullied. The impact of bullying can last a lifetime – from depression and anxiety to poor educational performance and much worse. We appreciate your past support for PACER’s National Bullying Prevention Center and we need your help now to make a difference for these children!

We hear from thousands of kids every year and they all need your help: The lonely teen who e-mails after midnight, unable to sleep because of the pain of being cyber-bullied day after day; the troubled parent who feels helpless trying to comfort his sobbing child who has endured unspeakable mental and physical cruelty; the teacher who is on the front lines every day and needs the right tools to deal with bullying in her school.

PACER is making a difference for these children, parents, and teachers across the country but we can’t do it without your help. Donate now if you believe every child deserves to feel safe at school.

Thank you for being a Champion for Children, both in the past and again now during National Bullying Prevention Month. We appreciate your support.
