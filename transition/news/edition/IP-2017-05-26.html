How to Think College:
Questions to Ask College Programs

This new tool from our partners at Think College, Conducting a College Search: Questions to Ask College Programs, helps parents of youth with intellectual and developmental disabilities determine the best college option for their son or daughter by asking the right questions. Read more (pdf).

Outside-The-Box College Accommodations for Students with Mental Health Conditions

This helpful new tip sheet from the University of Massachusetts Medical School’s Transitions Research and Training Center can help students with mental health conditions think “outside-the-box” in order to get the educational accommodations needed for their unique struggles. Read more.
